Recipe for Lasagna

A Timeless Italian Classic

Lasagna is a rich and flavorful Italian dish known for its layers of pasta, cheese, savory sauce, and sometimes meat or vegetables. Originating from Italy, this dish has become a favorite worldwide, with each region adding its unique twist. Traditional lasagna is made with sheets of pasta layered between a hearty meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel , and generous amounts of cheese, typically mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan. Baked to perfection, it results in a comforting, cheesy delight with a crispy, golden top. Whether enjoyed in a classic Italian style or with creative variations like vegetarian or seafood lasagna, this dish remains a beloved comfort food perfect for gatherings and family meals.
